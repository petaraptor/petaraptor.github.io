Snow Angel

Eliska and Jana, two girls blessed with Hellenistic beauty, live in a snowbound, desolate, eastern-European village. Here they while away their days dreaming of escape from their boring and poverty ridden lives. Luckily for them they possess the only means of escape from such a life - their youthful, Slavic beauty. Unfortunately it is not only the fashion world which desires these delectable attributes... 'Snow Angel' deals with issues such as the growing problem of sex trafficking and the exploitation of women in Europe whilst also further exploring and expanding upon the director's views on the Philosophy of Beauty. The film has hints of an underlying Faery Tale and will utilize this imagery throughout.
