ICBM - Inter-Continental Bunker Mission

Documentary, Feature Film, 83′ Stereo Sweden.
Post Production, 2022.
Directed by Julian Vogel, produced by Vogelperspektiv.
More info here.


This feature documentary explores preppers, people preparing for nuclear disaster. The atomic blast in the film is probably the most over-the-top thing I've ever done, it was awesome. The role of sound in this film is to guide the flow of the film, most interviews are as-is, but the transitions between them carries the most sound-design. Thereäs a selective use of atmospheres to convey a growing sense of claustrophobia as two friends attempt to live in a bunker.
