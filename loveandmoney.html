Love & Money

Fiction, Theatre Play, 100′ Stereo CZ
Original Score, Live Performance, 2020.
Written by Dennis Kelly, Directed by Milan Sebo.
More info here.



In this play sound is prominent throughout. The design utilises principles of psychoacoustics to at times extend the stage further than the room, as well to create a sense of claustophobia. These have been designed for live manipulation and performance in mind so that every performance is unique.

Atmospheric sounds are performed during the scenes with a combination of 4 track loops, samplers and synthesizer.

Transitions between scenes rely on a mixture of composed stems as well live patching.
