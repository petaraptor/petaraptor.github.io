The Mushroom Picker's Daughter (prequel)

Alois, a dying man and avid mushroom picker, awaits the return of his young daughter, Katerina, whom he has sent to the woods to gather the "Witches' Wart". This rare and specially cultivated mushroom will, she has been told, cure him of all his ailments and put an end to his suffering. What happens next will be a defining point in young Katerina's life and awakens something in her she never knew existed.
