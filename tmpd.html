The Mushroom Picker's Daughter (prequel)

Fiction, Short-Film, ca 13′ Stereo CZ-UK.
Location Sound, Post Production, & Original Score, 2017.
Directed by Will Thomas Freeman, produced by Dantalion Films.
More info here.



A few unusual processes were used in this project. All addictional sounds were recorded on location and processed heavily to achieve a "cut from sound-library" aesthetic. The mix is on the loud side, aimed for laptop speakers, phones, general internet delivery.

Russian folk music was sourced on vinyl to add an element of instability. My original score started off as remixes of said recordings.
You can hear the piece 'Sexy Stupid Dead' on my album 'Moments in Time'.
