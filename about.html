Petar is a Serbian - Swedish Sound Designer & Composer.


From the industrial forests of Södertälje. He taught himself filmmaking from an early age, and music on a broken upright piano. You'll find his work mainly in short films and plays. For the past 4 years you could also find him teaching sound-to-picture at Prague Film School.


Petar performs live music for Theatre and the solo ambient gigs. He expanded into surround editing and mixing for the film Sonntag. You can find him on the field recording production audio but he mainly works in post-production. From his home-studio.


There is always a desire for motion and vicera in his art. An obession with Noise, handheld Cinematography, & Sculptures with sharp edges. Aswell as a need to find beauty in the forgotten.


If you feel the same feel free to contact him in English, Swedish, or Serbian.
