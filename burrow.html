Burrow

Fiction, Theatre Play, 1 ACT 50′ Mono CZ.
Original Score, Live Performance, 2020



This one acter premiered in Prague during lighter COVID measures alongside another DonkeyDrop Theatre production 'The Edge', it was concieved during a state wide quarantine earlier in the year. Burrow starts with two abducted people who in their predicament let hate sidetrack their goals. The live aspect of the sound involved simple live routing and manipulating of predesigned samples.

One track from this show is available on my album 'Out Of Time'.
